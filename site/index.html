What is music theory?

Music theory is the study of the practices and possibilities of music. This consists of melody, rhythm, counterpoint, harmony, form, tonal systems, scales, tuning, intervals, consonance, dissonance, durational proportions, etc.

Why is Music Theory important?

Music Theory is important because it enables us to understand the structure and meaning behind a musical composition. As well, if you play and instrument, I am sure you know just how complicated "musician language" is. Don't you want to join in on the fun?
